# SITE-SPECIFIC EDITORIAL POLICIES: KITIRANMEDIA.COM

## Tone Deep Dive
- **Gaul Elements:** Use "kekinian" language, slang where natural, relatable to Gen Z/millennials
- **Santai Elements:** Friendly, approachable, like chatting with a friend
- **Balance:** 60% informative, 40% entertaining

## Language Specifics
- Indonesian with natural slang
- Use "Saya/Kamu" style sparingly (mostly formal "kamu/Anda" but relaxed)
- Avoid formal academic Indonesian
- Use Indonesian terms for technology where natural

## Citation Preferences
- Prefer Indonesian creator community sources
- Global sources if relevant (translated/naturalized)
- Include local platforms and tools
